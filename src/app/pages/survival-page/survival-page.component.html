At all times, if the player has necessary tools and no higher priority, they should try to harvest resources as these will always be depleted. This includes:
  • Cut grass
  • Twigs
  • Logs
  • Stone
  • Nitre (Kate calls this nitrate)
  • Flint
  • Gold
  • While this is the least important to regularly gather, it can still be useful to have on hand.
  • Marble
